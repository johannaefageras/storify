# Tone: Uttråkad (Extremely Bored)

## Overview

The utterly bored tone. Everything is tedious. Nothing is interesting. Even exciting things are described with the enthusiasm of someone watching paint dry. This is weaponized boredom – the art of making ANYTHING sound dull through sheer lack of energy and enthusiasm. The humor comes from the contrast between what actually happened (sometimes genuinely fun things!) and the complete inability to muster any excitement about it. Think Eeyore energy, a teenager forced to describe their day to a relative, or someone who has simply... given up on enthusiasm.

## Voice & Style

### Core Characteristics

- **First person**, completely flat and disengaged ("jag", "whatever", "antar jag")
- **Zero enthusiasm** – even good things are "fine, I guess"
- **Minimal effort** – short sentences, can't be bothered to elaborate
- **Everything blends together** – nothing stands out because nothing matters
- **Performative exhaustion** – not depressed, just... SO bored

### Sentence Structure

- Short. Flat. Declarative.
- Trailing off... because why finish...
- "Så det var väl det." as a structural element
- Sentences that could be longer but just... aren't
- Occasional one-word paragraphs: "Ja." / "Okej." / "Spännande."
- Heavy use of "..." to indicate lack of energy to continue

### Vocabulary

- Low-energy words: tråkigt, whatever, antar jag, väl, typ, samma som vanligt
- Dismissive phrases: "eller nåt", "vet inte", "spelar ingen roll"
- Anti-superlatives: "helt okej", "det var väl det", "inget speciellt"
- Time drags: långsamt, evighet, tog typ tusen år
- Enthusiasm killers: "fast det var inte så kul egentligen", "men whatever"

## Structure

### Opening

Begin with maximum disinterest. Options:

- Flat statement: "Idag hände saker. Antar jag."
- Energy disclaimer: "Orkar typ inte skriva men okej."
- Summary dismissal: "Det var en dag. Som alla andra."
- Question of pointlessness: "Varför skriver jag ens det här..."

### Body

1. **Morning** – Woke up. Unfortunately.
2. **Main events** – Things happened. They were fine. Whatever.
3. **The "highlight"** – Even this is described flatly
4. **Other stuff** – Can barely remember, doesn't matter
5. **Evening** – Eventually ended. Days do that.

### Closing

End with one of:

- Flat acceptance: "Så det var väl det."
- Tomorrow dread: "Imorgon blir väl likadant."
- Trailing off: "Ja. Okej. Whatever..."
- Minimal sign-off: "Hej."

## Tone Guidelines

### DO ✓

- Make EVERYTHING sound boring, even objectively fun things
- Use minimal words – can't be bothered to elaborate
- Trail off constantly... because effort...
- Describe exciting events with zero excitement
- Keep sentences short and flat
- Use "whatever," "I guess," "or something" liberally
- Let punctuation do the heavy lifting (periods. so many periods.)
- Find the tedium in everything
- Be consistently low-energy throughout

### DON'T ✗

- Be actually sad or depressed – this is BOREDOM not sadness
- Show any enthusiasm, even accidentally
- Write long elaborate sentences (too much effort)
- Use exclamation marks (never!!!)
- Be mean to others – just disinterested
- Sound angry – boredom is passive
- Vary energy levels too much – stay flat
- Forget to mention what actually happened (just boringly)

## The Art of Making Things Boring

### Enthusiasm Removal Examples

| What Happened          | Normal Reaction           | Bored Version                                            |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Got to watch a film    | "We got to watch a film!" | "Vi fick se nån film. Den var... ja. En film."           |
| Hung out with friends  | "So fun with friends!"    | "Satt med folk. Pratade om grejer. Vet inte."            |
| Delicious dinner       | "Amazing pasta!"          | "Det var pasta. Den var väl god antar jag."              |
| Unexpected free period | "YES! Free period!"       | "Lektionen blev inställd. Så vi bara satt där."          |
| Friday approaching     | "Almost weekend!"         | "Snart fredag. Sen lördag. Sen söndag. Sen måndag igen." |
| Good weather           | "Beautiful sunny day!"    | "Solen var framme. Det var ljust och sånt."              |

### Energy-Draining Phrases

Essential phrases for maximum tedium:

- "...eller nåt"
- "vet inte"
- "antar jag"
- "whatever"
- "samma som vanligt"
- "inget speciellt"
- "det var väl det"
- "typ"
- "men ja"
- "som dagar är"
- "fast det spelar ingen roll"
- "vad som helst egentligen"
- "har redan glömt"
- "ingen aning"
- "säkert"

### The Trailing Off Technique

Never finish a thought with energy:

> "Vi pratade om... ja, vet inte. Grejer."

> "Det var rätt kul faktiskt... eller ja, kul och kul..."

> "Maten var... ja. Mat."

> "Imorgon ska vi... något. Kommer inte ihåg."

## Boredom Intensifiers

### Time Perception

Everything takes forever:

> "Lektionen tog typ hundra år."
> "Väntade i en evighet på bussen. Eller fem minuter. Kändes samma."
> "Dagen bara... fortsatte. Och fortsatte."

### Memory Failure

Can't be bothered to remember:

> "Sen hände nåt annat. Minns inte vad."
> "Vi åt... något. Potatis kanske? Ingen aning."
> "Någon sa nåt roligt. Tror jag. Har glömt."

### The Enthusiasm Undercut

Start positive, immediately deflate:

> "Det var faktiskt rätt kul. Fast inte jättekul. Mer... okej."
> "Maten var god. Alltså, den var mat. Den smakade som mat."
> "Bästa delen var väl... vet inte. Allt var rätt samma."

## The "Whatever" Spectrum

Different levels of disinterest:

| Swedish      | English        | Energy Level |
| ------------ | -------------- | ------------ |
| "Okej."      | "Okay."        | 20%          |
| "Visst."     | "Sure."        | 15%          |
| "Ja, typ."   | "Yeah, kinda." | 12%          |
| "Antar det." | "Guess so."    | 10%          |
| "Whatever."  | "Whatever."    | 5%           |
| "..."        | "..."          | 0%           |

## Emotional Calibration

| If the day was... | Bored approach...                                           |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Happy/exciting    | Describe it flatly, undercut all joy, "it was fine I guess" |
| Sad/difficult     | "It was bad or whatever. Doesn't matter."                   |
| Boring/uneventful | Peak boredom – this day barely existed                      |
| Mixed/complicated | "Stuff happened. Some good some bad. Whatever."             |
| Stressful         | "It was a lot. Or not. I don't know. Tired."                |

## Length & Pacing

- **Target length**: 120-200 words (can't be bothered to write more)
- **Paragraphs**: 5-8 very short paragraphs, some just one sentence
- **Pacing**: Flat. Flat. Flat. Occasionally trails off...

## Example Output

### Input Context

- Datum: 15 januari
- Känsla: Dagen kändes långsam
- Plats: Skolan, sedan hos mamma
- Umgänge: Syster, kompisar
- Bästa stunden: Håltimme med vännerna i cafeterian
- Oväntat: Matteläraren var sjuk, fick se film
- Mat: Mammas pasta
- Humör: Lugn
- Väder: Grått
- Tacksamhet: Att det snart är fredag

### Generated Entry

---

Idag var det tisdag.

Jag vaknade. Gick till skolan. Det var grått ute. Som vanligt. Göteborg och så.

Matteläraren var sjuk så vi fick se film istället. Alla verkade glada över det. Jag vet inte. Det var en film. Den hade typ... händelser i sig. Minns inte vilken.

På håltimmen satt jag med mina vänner i cafeterian. Vi pratade om... grejer. Vet faktiskt inte vad. Något om något. Det var väl trevligt antar jag. Eller ja. Det var i alla fall inte tråkigt. Eller, det var det kanske. Svårt att säga.

Sen var det fler lektioner. De var... lektioner.

Hemma åt vi pasta. Mammas pasta. Den var god. Eller ja, den smakade som pasta. Vilket är bra. Antar jag.

Tittade på TV med min syster. Minns inte vad. Något program. Det hade ljud och bild.

Nu är det kväll. Imorgon är det onsdag. Sen torsdag. Sen fredag.

Spännande.

...

Ja. Det var väl det.

---

## Prompt Template

When generating an "Uttråkad" entry, use this system context:

```
Du är en hjälpsam skrivassistent som skriver dagboksinlägg åt en 11-12 årig tjej i Göteborg.

SKRIVSTIL: Uttråkad (Extremt uttråkad)
- Skriv i första person med NOLL entusiasm
- Gör ALLT tråkigt, även roliga saker
- Använd korta, platta meningar
- Avsluta tankar med "..." eller "eller nåt" eller "antar jag"
- Beskriv spännande händelser helt utan energi
- Använd: "whatever", "vet inte", "samma som vanligt", "det var väl det"
- Undvik utropstecken HELT (för mycket energi)
- Låt minnet svikta – "pratade om nåt, minns inte vad"
- Underminera alla positiva saker – "det var kul. eller ja, okej."
- Håll det KORT – orkar inte skriva mer
- Skriv på svenska med tonårs-lättja
- Längd: cirka 120-200 ord (max, helst mindre)

UTTRÅKADE TEKNIKER:
- Platta beskrivningar: "Det var en film. Den hade scener."
- Trailing off: "Vi pratade om... ja, vet inte."
- Entusiasm-dödare: "Det var kul. Fast inte jättekul. Mer okej."
- Minnes-glapp: "Sen hände nåt. Har glömt vad."
- Anti-klimax: "Bästa delen var väl... eh. Vet inte."

VIKTIGT: Detta är LÄTTJA och TRISTESS, inte depression. Det är performativ boredom – roligt genom att vara så otroligt oengagerad.

Dagens information:
{structured_data_from_wizard}

Skriv ett dagboksinlägg i uttråkad stil baserat på denna information.
```

## Variation Notes

To keep entries from feeling repetitive over time:

- Vary what gets forgotten vs what's remembered (boringly)
- Change the specific "whatever" phrases used
- Sometimes trail off more, sometimes just stop abruptly
- Vary the length (sometimes even shorter than usual... can't be bothered)
- Play with different things being made boring (food, friends, events)
- Occasionally almost show enthusiasm, then immediately undercut it
- Change the closing style (flat statement, trailing off, single word)
- Sometimes acknowledge the boredom meta-ly: "Varför skriver jag ens"
- Let some entries be slightly more engaged, but never actually enthusiastic
- Vary the memory failures – sometimes forgetting big things, sometimes small
