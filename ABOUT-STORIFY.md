# Storify Brand & Product Brief For Blog Writing

Storify is a Swedish-first AI journaling app available at https://mystorify.se. It helps people write personal diary entries even when they do not have the time, energy, or words to start from a blank page.

The core idea is simple: the user gives Storify a few fragments about their day, and Storify turns those fragments into a polished diary entry in a chosen narrative voice. The app is private by default, playful in tone, and built around the belief that ordinary days are worth remembering.

## One-Sentence Description

Storify is a private AI diary that asks simple questions about your day and turns your answers into a memorable diary entry in one of many creative voices.

## Core Positioning

Storify is not a generic AI writing app. It is a memory-keeping product.

It should be described as:

- A private AI diary.
- A tool for capturing everyday life without blank-page pressure.
- A Swedish journaling app with personality.
- A way to turn small daily details into something worth saving.
- A playful but sincere writing companion.

Avoid describing Storify as:

- A productivity app.
- A therapy replacement.
- A generic chatbot.
- A “content generator.”
- A self-help app full of vague wellness language.

## Primary Audience

Storify is for people who want to keep a diary but rarely manage to write one consistently.

Common user motivations:

- “I want to remember my life better.”
- “I want to journal, but I never know what to write.”
- “I’m too tired at night to write a proper entry.”
- “My ordinary days disappear too quickly.”
- “I want something more personal than a notes app.”
- “I want a diary that feels fun, not like homework.”

The audience may include adults, teens, parents, students, busy professionals, reflective people, and anyone who likes the idea of preserving everyday memories.

## The Problem Storify Solves

The blank diary page is intimidating. Most people do not fail at journaling because they lack meaningful lives. They fail because “just start writing” is bad advice.

At the end of a normal day, people are tired. They may remember scattered moments: a conversation, a mood, a commute, a meal, a small win, an irritation, a funny detail. Turning that into a coherent diary entry takes effort.

Storify lowers that effort. It asks answerable questions, accepts fragments, and uses AI to shape them into a diary entry.

A good framing:
“Storify does not invent your life. It helps you notice and preserve the life you already had today.”

## Product Features

Storify has four writing modes:

1. AI-intervju
   The user chats with an AI interviewer that asks natural follow-up questions about the day. After the conversation, Storify generates a diary entry.

2. Steg-för-steg
   A guided wizard where the user answers structured questions about mood, energy, places, activities, people, wins, frustrations, food, music, reflections, and more.

3. Snabbläge
   A quick mode for capturing the day in under a minute. Good for low-energy days or users who want a fast ritual.

4. Skriv fritt
   A free-writing editor where the user writes in their own words and AI helps polish or refine the text without changing the meaning.

Other important features:

- Private journal for saved entries.
- Calendar view and writing streaks.
- Ability to edit saved entries.
- Ability to regenerate an entry in a different voice.
- Public share links for selected entries.
- Optional community sharing.
- PDF/export-style sharing utilities.
- Profile personalization.
- Theme, accent color, and font settings.
- Badges and light gamification.
- Weekly recap email and push reminder infrastructure.
- Blog with educational and reflective posts about journaling, memory, AI writing, and everyday life.

## Brand Voice

Storify’s own voice is Swedish, warm, direct, and lightly funny. It should feel human, clever, and a little irreverent without becoming snarky.

Good Storify tone:

- “Du har inte tid att skriva dagbok. Perfekt – det behöver du inte heller.”
- “Tom dagbokssida-panik är på riktigt.”
- “Börja bara skriva är ett värdelöst råd.”
- “Samma dag. Helt olika minne.”
- “Berätta i två minuter. Få tillbaka en text du faktiskt vill läsa om tio år.”

The voice should be:

- Clear.
- Swedish-first.
- Emotionally honest.
- Playful but grounded.
- Warm without being sugary.
- Helpful without sounding corporate.
- Funny without undermining the sincerity of journaling.

Avoid:

- Generic wellness clichés.
- Corporate SaaS language.
- Overly polished marketing fluff.
- Therapy claims.
- “Unlock your potential” style self-help.
- Treating AI as magic.
- Overpromising emotional transformation.

## Privacy Positioning

Privacy is central. Storify is a diary, so trust matters.

Important points:

- Entries are private by default.
- Users choose what to share.
- Community sharing is optional.
- Public links are explicit user actions.
- Storify should be framed as a personal memory space, not a social network first.
- Avoid suggesting that private diary content is public or automatically shared.

Suggested phrasing:
“Din dagbok är din.”
“Allt är privat som standard.”
“Du delar bara det du själv väljer.”

## Blog Style Guidelines

Blog posts should be written in Swedish unless explicitly requested otherwise.

The writing should feel:

- Useful.
- Personal.
- Warm.
- Slightly witty.
- Concrete.
- Easy to read.
- Search-friendly, but not SEO-stuffed.

Prefer:

- Short paragraphs.
- Clear subheadings.
- Practical examples.
- Realistic scenarios.
- Specific journaling prompts.
- Friendly, direct address using “du.”
- A natural connection back to Storify.

Avoid:

- Long academic explanations.
- Empty motivational language.
- Overusing AI buzzwords.
- Treating journaling as a moral obligation.
- Saying everyone “should” journal.
- Making mental health claims.
- Saying Storify replaces therapy, coaching, or professional help.
- Generic conclusions like “start your journey today.”

## Suggested Calls To Action

Use soft, relevant CTAs. Storify should not sound pushy.

Good CTAs:

- “Vill du slippa börja från en tom sida? Testa Storify.”
- “Berätta några ord om din dag och låt Storify göra resten.”
- “Skriv dagens första minne på två minuter.”
- “Testa att låta en AI-intervju hjälpa dig hitta vad dagen egentligen handlade om.”
- “Samma dag kan bli en klassisk dagbok, en kattmonolog eller ett sportreferat.”

Avoid hard-sales language:

- “Sign up now!”
- “Unlock your best self.”
- “Transform your life.”
- “Revolutionize your journaling.”

## Key Message

Storify helps people remember their lives without demanding that they become disciplined diary writers. It turns small, messy, everyday fragments into diary entries that feel personal, readable, and worth coming back to.

The best blog content should make readers feel:
“I actually could keep a diary if it worked like this.”
