# Tone: IKEA-manual

## Overview

The IKEA assembly instructions tone. The day is documented as a step-by-step assembly guide for a product called DAGËN or LIVËT or some other suspiciously Swedish-sounding flat-pack item — complete with numbered steps, ambiguous diagrams described in text, parts lists, Allen key metaphors, and the quiet existential crisis of finding three screws left over at the end. Every activity is a step in the assembly process, every person is a required component, every feeling is a troubleshooting scenario. The humor comes from the absurd precision of instructional language applied to the messy, unassemblable experience of being alive. Think BILLY bookshelf meets Monday morning meets the universal experience of staring at a wordless instruction sheet and questioning your life choices. Very Swedish, very deadpan, very satisfying when all the parts finally come together.

## Voice & Style

### Core Characteristics

- **Impersonal instructional voice** — "Placera KROPP i upprätt position", never "I" or "me"
- **Product naming convention** — the day, feelings, and objects get IKEA-style names in CAPS (DAGËN, ENERGI, FRUKOST, LUNCHËN)
- **Step numbering** — everything is sequential, numbered, procedural
- **Deadpan practical** — emotions described as mechanical functions or assembly problems
- **Diagram references** — "(se fig. 3)", "(se bild A)" pointing to illustrations that don't exist
- **Warnings and notes** — ⚠️ VIKTIGT and 💡 TIPS scattered throughout
- **Parts list mentality** — people, items, and feelings are listed as components with article numbers

### Sentence Structure

- Short, imperative instructions: "Applicera KAFFE. Vänta 5-10 min."
- Conditional troubleshooting: "Om MOTIVATION saknas, se felsökningsguide (s. 14)."
- Numbered steps with sub-steps: "Steg 3a: Lokalisera JACKA."
- Parenthetical references: "(medföljer ej)", "(säljs separat)", "(se fig. 2)"
- Passive, impersonal constructions: "FRUKOST intas.", "Transport genomförs."
- Warnings as asides: "⚠️ OBS: Ej applicera SOCIALT UMGÄNGE innan KAFFE har absorberats."

### Vocabulary

- Assembly language: montera, applicera, placera, fäst, justera, kontrollera
- Components: del, komponent, ingår, medföljer, medföljer ej, säljs separat
- Product codes: artikelnummer, REF, modell, serie
- Quality control: kontrollera, verifiera, säkerställ, avvikelse
- Troubleshooting: om... uppstår, se felsökningsguide, kontakta support
- Completion: slutresultat, montering klar, driftsklar, godkänd
- Disclaimer language: tillverkaren ansvarar ej, resultat kan variera, garanti gäller ej

## Structure

### Opening

Begin with a product header and parts list. Options:

- Product sheet: "**DAGËN** — Tisdagsmodell / Art.nr: 2026-01-15 / 1 st dag, komplett med medföljande väder (grått)"
- Assembly start: "Grattis till ditt köp av DAGËN (tisdag, januarimodell). Läs igenom alla steg innan montering påbörjas."
- Parts inventory: "Kontrollera att samtliga delar finns med innan montering påbörjas. Se dellista (fig. A)."
- Warning label: "⚠️ VIKTIGT: Montera ej DAGËN utan tillräcklig SÖMN. Tillverkaren ansvarar ej för resultat vid bristfällig vila."

### Body

1. **Dellista** — Parts/components needed for the day (people, items, weather, energy levels)
2. **Steg 1-3: Morgonmontering** — Waking, breakfast, getting ready as assembly steps
3. **Steg 4-6: Huvudmontering** — School/work as the primary construction phase
4. **Steg 7-8: Tilläggsmoduler** — Social moments, unexpected events as optional add-ons
5. **Steg 9-10: Kvällsmontering** — Dinner, evening, winding down as final assembly
6. **Slutkontroll** — End-of-day quality check and leftover parts inventory

### Closing

End with one of:

- Quality inspection: "Slutkontroll genomförd. DAGËN godkänd. 3 skruvar över. Normalt."
- Product rating: "Montering: ★★★☆☆. Stabil konstruktion trots avsaknad av sol."
- Leftover parts: "Kvarvarande delar: 1 st outnyttjad MOTIVATION, 2 st ÅNGEST (okänd källa). Kan kasseras."
- Next product teaser: "Nästa produkt i serien: ONSDAGËN. Montering påbörjas 07:00."
- Warranty disclaimer: "Garanti gäller ej vid felaktig användning av HELG. Resultat kan variera."

## Tone Guidelines

### DO ✓

- Name EVERYTHING in IKEA-style caps: FRUKOST, ENERGI, KOMPISAR, LÄXA, SOFFAN
- Number all steps — the day is a sequential process
- Include a parts list with article numbers and "(medföljer ej)" notes
- Reference non-existent diagrams: "(se fig. 4)", "(se bild B)"
- Add ⚠️ warnings and 💡 tips throughout
- Treat emotions as mechanical states: "HUMÖR inställt på LUGN"
- Describe people as components: "SYSTER (1 st, medföljande)"
- Include troubleshooting for when things don't go as planned
- Keep the deadpan instructional voice completely straight-faced
- End with leftover parts — there are ALWAYS leftover parts
- Let warmth seep through via the absurdity — describing a hug as "Applicera KRAM (2-4 sek)" is funny AND sweet

### DON'T ✗

- Break character into normal diary voice — commit to the manual format
- Make it actually confusing to read — the events should be clear through the instructions
- Overload with too many product codes and numbers — use them for flavor, not clutter
- Forget the actual day — every step corresponds to a real event
- Be cold or mechanical without humor — the tone is warm _through_ its absurdity
- Make the IKEA names too forced — they should feel natural in the format
- Skip the parts list — it's a signature element
- Forget warnings and tips — they carry the best jokes
- Make every step the same length — vary between quick steps and detailed ones
- Lose the Swedish IKEA flavor — this tone is inherently, distinctly Swedish

## IKEA-manual Techniques

### The Parts List (Dellista)

Open with an inventory of the day's components:

> **DELLISTA — DAGËN (Tisdag 15/1)**
>
> | Del           | Antal | Artikelnr | Status       |
> | ------------- | ----- | --------- | ------------ |
> | KROPP         | 1 st  | KR-001    | ✓            |
> | ENERGI        | ~40%  | EN-040    | ⚠️ låg       |
> | FRUKOST       | 1 st  | FR-STD    | ✓            |
> | KOMPISAR      | 3 st  | KO-003    | ✓            |
> | MOTIVATION    | 1 st  | MO-001    | saknas       |
> | VÄDER (grått) | 1 st  | VÄ-GRÅ    | ✓            |
> | INSOMNINGSKIT | 1 st  | SE-022    | medföljer ej |

### The Assembly Step

Describe activities as numbered instructions:

> **Steg 3: TRANSPORT**
>
> Förflytta KROPP till hållplats. Invänta BUSS (kan ta 3-12 min, resultat varierar).
> Placera dig i SITS. Applicera HÖRLURAR (säljs separat).
>
> ⚠️ OBS: Om BUSS ej anländer inom 15 min, se felsökningsguide (s. 7).

### The Diagram Reference

Point to illustrations that obviously don't exist:

> "Montera RYGGSÄCK på KROPP (se fig. 2 för korrekt axelposition)."
> "Placera TALLRIK framför dig. Applicera PASTA (se bild C för rekommenderad mängd)."
> "LEENDE monteras automatiskt vid kontakt med KOMPISAR (se fig. 5)."

### The Warning Box

Drop important safety information:

> ⚠️ **VIKTIGT:** Använd ej TELEFON under LEKTION. Risk för konfiskering. Tillverkaren ansvarar ej för konsekvenser.

> ⚠️ **VARNING:** ENERGI-nivå under 20% kan leda till instabil HUMÖR-konstruktion. Fyll på med FIKA vid behov.

### The Tip Box

Offer helpful assembly advice:

> 💡 **TIPS:** PASTA smakar bättre om den tillagas av MAMMA-enhet. Resultatet kan ej replikeras med egen MATLAGNING.

> 💡 **TIPS:** Om HÅLTIMME uppstår oväntat — utnyttja för SOCIAL MONTERING. Dessa tillfällen är sällsynta.

### The Troubleshooting Section

Handle problems with technical calm:

> **Om MOTIVATION saknas:**
>
> 1. Kontrollera att SÖMN (min. 7 tim) har genomförts
> 2. Applicera KAFFE (1-2 koppar)
> 3. Om problemet kvarstår: FREDAG närmar sig. Invänta.

> **Om HUMÖR är instabilt:**
>
> 1. Lokalisera närmaste KOMPIS eller HUSDJUR
> 2. Applicera SAMTAL eller KRAM (2-4 sek)
> 3. Om problemet kvarstår, kontakta MAMMA-support

### The Leftover Parts

There are always mysterious parts left over:

> **Kvarvarande delar efter montering:**
>
> - 1 st ENERGI (oförbrukad — källa okänd)
> - 2 st TANKAR (överblivna, passar ingen specifik steg)
> - 3 st SKRUVAR (okänt ursprung, förmodligen viktiga)
>
> Detta är normalt. Kassera ej — kan behövas vid framtida montering.

### The Product Rating

End with a consumer-style assessment:

> **PRODUKTRECENSION — DAGËN 15/1:**
> Montering: ★★★☆☆
> Stabilitet: ★★★★☆
> Design: ★★☆☆☆ (grått väder drar ner helhetsintrycket)
> Medföljer: Pasta, kompisar, oväntat filmvisning
> Medföljer ej: Sol, motivation, mattelärare

## The Hidden Warmth

The comedy of the IKEA-manual tone masks genuine feeling. The warmth comes through in the absurdity of describing human experiences in product language:

> "Applicera KRAM på MAMMA-enhet. Håll i 3-5 sekunder. Notera: denna komponent ger obegränsat med TRYGGHET och kräver inget underhåll."

> "KOMPISAR monterade sig spontant runt bordet under HÅLTIMMË. Ingen instruktion behövdes. Vissa konstruktioner bygger sig själva."

> "💡 TIPS: DAGËN behöver inte vara perfekt monterad för att vara fullt funktionell. Ojämna kanter är en del av designen."

> "Kvarvarande TACKSAMHET efter montering: 1 st, stor. Ursprung: att det snart är HËLGEN. Kassera ej."

## Length & Pacing

- **Target length**: 200-300 words
- **Paragraphs**: 6-10 short blocks mixing steps, lists, warnings, and tips
- **Pacing**: Clipped, instructional, with rhythm created by alternating steps, notes, and asides

## Variation Notes

To keep entries from feeling repetitive over time:

- Vary the product name and series — DAGËN, LIVËT, TISDAGËN, VECKAN, ÖVERLËVA
- Change which events become detailed steps vs. quick instructions
- Rotate the opening format — product sheet, assembly start, warning label, parts inventory
- Include different types of warnings and tips — safety, practical, philosophical
- Vary the parts list contents — sometimes focused on people, sometimes feelings, sometimes objects
- Change the troubleshooting scenarios — motivation, energy, mood, social anxiety
- Play with the leftover parts at the end — sometimes funny, sometimes poignant
- Vary the product rating and what criteria are used
- Sometimes include a "REKLAMATION" (complaint/return) section for bad days
- Occasionally reference other IKEA "products" in the series: "Se även: SÖNDAGËN (vilmodul)"
- Change diagram figure numbers and what they supposedly illustrate
- Vary the article numbers and their formats
- Sometimes a step fails and requires improvised solutions — "Steg 5 kunde ej genomföras enligt plan. Alternativ montering tillämpades."
- Let some days have cleaner assembly (all parts fit) and others be chaotic builds (extra parts, missing screws, improvised solutions)
- Occasionally include a "KUNDRECENSION" from the user as closing
